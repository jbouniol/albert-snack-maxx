### 🏢 Snack Max — Company Background

**Current State:**

- 2,000 vending machines across Paris universities and metro system
- 100 employees: 40 stockers + 60 office staff (buyers, finance, HR, customer service)
- Cash-only machines (coins and bills) with manual number-letter selection
- Declining business since peak profitability

**Financial Trajectory:** 

- Founded 1985, profitable by 1990
- Revenue: **50M€** (1990) → **25M€** (2026) — revenue cut in half
- Profit: **10M€** (1990) → **4M€** (2026)
- Goals (2029): Revenue **50M€** | Profit **15M€**

**Key Problems:** 

- Main costs: (1) People (2) Machines + maintenance (3) Food inventory
- Machines frequently need repair due to malfunctions and customer frustration
- Stocking on fixed schedule — inefficient (machines empty or overfilled)
- Product selection based on **supplier pricing**, not customer demand
- Prices higher than competitors due to inefficient operations

**Strategic Goal:** 


- 💡 Shift from supplier-driven selection to **customer demand-driven** selection
- No budget constraints — treat as startup with access to investment capital
